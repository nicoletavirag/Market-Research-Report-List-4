<p><p>Induction Heating Generators are classified into two main types based on frequency: Medium Frequency Generators and High Frequency Generators. Medium Frequency Generators typically operate at frequencies ranging from 1 kHz to 10 kHz, making them suitable for a wide range of industrial applications. On the other hand, High Frequency Generators operate at frequencies above 10 kHz, offering more precise and efficient heating for specialized applications such as soldering and brazing. Both types of generators play a crucial role in various industries for heating metal components rapidly and uniformly.</p></p>
