<p><ul><li>Wire to Wire Connector</li><li>Wire to Board Connector</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Commercial vehicle harness market can be divided into two main types: wire to wire connector and wire to board connector. Wire to wire connectors are used to connect individual wires together within a harness, allowing for communication between different components of the vehicle. On the other hand, wire to board connectors are used to link wires to a circuit board, enabling electrical signals to be transmitted between the harness and the vehicle's electronic system. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring the proper functioning of commercial vehicles.</p></p>
