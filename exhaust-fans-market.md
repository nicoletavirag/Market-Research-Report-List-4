<p><p>Exhaust fans are commonly categorized into different types based on their design and functionality. Centrifugal exhaust fans are ideal for moving large volumes of air in industrial settings. Inline centrifugal exhaust fans are compact and suitable for applications where space is limited. Radial exhaust fans are efficient in handling high air volumes at low pressures. Tubeaxial and vaneaxial exhaust fans are specifically designed for ventilation systems where air needs to be moved through ducts. Each type of exhaust fan serves a specific purpose and is used in various industries for optimal air circulation.</p></p>
