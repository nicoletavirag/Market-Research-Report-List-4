<p><p>The visible fiber laser market consists of two main types: continuous wave (CW) and pulsed. Continuous wave lasers emit a continuous beam of light, making them suitable for tasks like cutting and welding. Pulsed lasers, on the other hand, emit short bursts of laser light, allowing for precise control and less heat generation, making them ideal for applications like marking and engraving. Both types cater to different needs within the visible fiber laser market, providing versatility and diversity in laser technology.</p></p>
