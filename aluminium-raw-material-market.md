<p><p>Aluminium raw material refers to the primary input materials required for the production of aluminium, including bauxite, alumina, and carbon anodes. Alumina is extracted from bauxite through the Bayer process, while carbon anodes are used in the electrolysis process to produce aluminium.</p><p>The aluminium raw material market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 14% during the forecast period. This growth can be attributed to the increasing demand for aluminium across various industries such as automotive, aerospace, construction, and packaging. The rising focus on sustainability and energy efficiency has also boosted the demand for aluminium, as it is a lightweight and recyclable material.</p><p>One of the latest trends in the aluminium raw material market is the shift towards renewable energy sources for aluminium production.
