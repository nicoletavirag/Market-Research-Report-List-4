<p><strong>The Electric Power Assisted Steering (EPAS) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>C-EPS</li><li>P-EPS</li><li>R-EPS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electric Power Assisted Steering (EPAS) systems are categorized into three main types: Column-Electric Power Steering (C-EPS), Pinion-Electric Power Steering (P-EPS), and Rack-Electric Power Steering (R-EPS). C-EPS systems have the motor mounted on the steering column, P-EPS have the motor mounted on the steering gear, and R-EPS have the motor mounted on the rack. Each type offers unique benefits such as compact design in C-EPS, improved response in P-EPS, and enhanced precision in R-EPS, catering to different vehicle requirements.</p></p>
