<p><p>The automotive cable materials market includes various types such as PVC, XLPE, TPU, PPE, and others. PVC (polyvinyl chloride) is a common choice due to its durability and flexibility. XLPE (cross-linked polyethylene) offers higher temperature resistance and insulation properties. TPU (thermoplastic polyurethane) is known for its abrasion resistance and flexibility. PPE (polyphenylene ether) provides excellent electrical properties. Other materials in the market include materials like rubber and silicone, each offering unique characteristics for different automotive cable applications.</p></p>
