<p><p>Bell furnaces are commonly used in heat treatment processes for metal parts. They are available in two primary market types: electrically heated and gas-fired. Electrically heated furnaces use electricity to generate heat for the treatment process, offering precise temperature control and uniform heating. Gas-fired furnaces, on the other hand, use natural gas or propane as a fuel source for heat generation. Both types of furnaces are widely used in industries such as automotive, aerospace, and manufacturing for various heat treatment applications.</p></p>
