<p><p>Mobile CRM can be categorized into two main market types: on-premise and cloud-based. On-premise CRM systems are installed and maintained on the company's own servers, providing complete control and customization but requiring higher upfront costs and IT resources. On the other hand, cloud-based CRM systems are hosted and accessed over the internet, offering flexibility, scalability, and cost-effectiveness. Companies can choose the market type that best fits their needs and resources to effectively manage their customer relationships on-the-go through mobile devices.</p></p>
