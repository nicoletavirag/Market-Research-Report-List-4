<p><p>Chiller systems are used for cooling applications in various industries. The market is divided into different types based on the technology used like Centrifugal Chillers, Scroll Chiller, Screw Chiller, and Others. Centrifugal chillers use rotating impellers to compress refrigerant, Scroll chillers use scroll compressors for efficient cooling, Screw chillers use rotating screws to compress refrigerant, and other types may include absorption chillers or magnetic bearing chillers. Each type has its advantages and is chosen based on specific cooling requirements.</p></p>
