<p><p>A Diaphragm Tank is a type of pressure vessel that is used to store and regulate water pressure in a water system. It consists of a flexible membrane (diaphragm) that separates the water from the air inside the tank. This allows for the water to be pressurized without coming into direct contact with the air.</p><p>The Diaphragm Tank Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 14.6% during the forecast period.
