<p><ul><li>Regular-Tow Carbon Fiber</li><li>Large-Tow Carbon Fiber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Regular-Tow Carbon Fiber refers to carbon fibers with a standard tow size, typically ranging from 3K to 24K. This type of carbon fiber is commonly used in industries such as aerospace, automotive, and sporting goods due to its high strength-to-weight ratio. On the other hand, Large-Tow Carbon Fiber has a tow size greater than 24K, making it more suitable for applications requiring higher stiffness and impact resistance. Industries such as wind energy and industrial equipment often use Large-Tow Carbon Fiber for their products.</p></p>
