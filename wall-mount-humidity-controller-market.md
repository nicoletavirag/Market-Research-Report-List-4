<p><strong>The Wall Mount Humidity Controller Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Automatic</li><li>Toggle</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Wall mount humidity controllers come in two main market types - automatic and toggle. </p><p>Automatic controllers are designed to continuously monitor the humidity levels in a space and automatically adjust the settings to maintain the desired humidity level. This makes it a convenient option for those who want hands-free control over their humidity levels.</p><p>Toggle controllers, on the other hand, require manual adjustments to set and maintain the desired humidity level. While not as hands-free as automatic controllers, toggle controllers are often simpler and more cost-effective options for those who prefer more control over their humidity settings.</p></p>
