There are two main types of governors on the market: brush type and brushless type. Brush type governors use brushes to transfer power to the motor, while brushless type governors utilize electronic circuits for power transmission. Brush type governors are typically more affordable and easier to maintain, while brushless type governors offer higher efficiency and greater longevity. Both types cater to different market needs and preferences within the UAV industry.</p></p>
