<p><p>Printed tape market is categorized into three main types based on adhesive composition - Acrylic Tape, Hot Melt Tape, and Natural Rubber Tape. Acrylic tape is known for its durable and long-lasting adhesive properties, making it suitable for a wide range of applications. Hot melt tape offers quick adhesion and strong holding power, ideal for heavy-duty packaging needs. Natural rubber tape provides excellent adhesion to a variety of surfaces and is preferred for sealing boxes in challenging environments. Each type of tape caters to specific requirements in the packaging industry.</p></p>
