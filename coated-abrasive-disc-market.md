<p><ul><li>Conventional Abrasive</li><li>Super Abrasive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Coated abrasive discs can be categorized into two main types: Conventional Abrasive and Super Abrasive discs. Conventional Abrasive discs are made of materials like aluminum oxide or silicon carbide and are commonly used for general metalworking applications. On the other hand, Super Abrasive discs are made of materials like diamond or cubic boron nitride and are designed for high-precision grinding tasks. Super Abrasive discs offer superior durability and cutting performance compared to Conventional Abrasive discs.</p></p>
