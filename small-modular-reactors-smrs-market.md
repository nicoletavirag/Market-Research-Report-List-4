The two main market types for SMRs are Thermal-neutron Reactors and Fast-neutron Reactors. Thermal-neutron reactors use slow-moving neutrons to sustain the nuclear chain reaction, while fast-neutron reactors use fast-moving neutrons. Both types of reactors have their own advantages and disadvantages, with thermal-neutron reactors being more common and fast-neutron reactors offering the potential for increased efficiency and reduced waste.</p></p>
