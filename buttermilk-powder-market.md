<p><strong>The Buttermilk Powder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spray Dried Buttermilk Powder</li><li>Atmospheric Roller Dried Buttermilk Powder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Buttermilk powder is available in two main types: spray dried buttermilk powder and atmospheric roller dried buttermilk powder. Spray dried buttermilk powder is produced by spraying the liquid buttermilk into hot air to evaporate the moisture, resulting in a fine powder. On the other hand, atmospheric roller dried buttermilk powder is made by spreading a thin layer of buttermilk on a heated surface, where it is dried and then scraped off as a powder. Both types of buttermilk powder have distinct characteristics and applications in the food industry.</p></p>
