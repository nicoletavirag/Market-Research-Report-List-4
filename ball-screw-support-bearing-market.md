<p><strong>The Ball Screw Support Bearing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Row</li><li>Double Row</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ball screw support bearings can be categorized into two main types: Single Row and Double Row bearings. </p><p>Single Row ball screw support bearings have a single row of balls arranged in a circular raceway to support axial loads, while Double Row bearings have two rows of balls to increase load capacity and provide enhanced rigidity. </p><p>Both types are commonly used in machinery with linear motion systems to ensure smooth and precise movement of the ball screw assembly. The choice between Single Row and Double Row bearings depends on the specific requirements of the application.</p></p>
