<p><p>Intelligent Dosimetry Equipment market consists of different types of technology, including Thermoluminescent Dosimetry (TLD) and Optically Stimulated Luminescence (OSL). TLD measures radiation exposure by detecting light emitted when certain materials are heated. OSL, on the other hand, measures radiation exposure by using light stimulation to release stored energy. Both technologies play a crucial role in monitoring radiation levels in various industries such as healthcare, nuclear power, and manufacturing, ensuring the safety of workers and the public.</p></p>
