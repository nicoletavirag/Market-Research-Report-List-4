<p><p>Iron core reactors are devices used in power systems to regulate voltage and maintain stability. There are two main types of iron core reactors - dry type and oil immersed type. Dry type reactors use air as the cooling medium, making them more suitable for indoor installations where fire safety is a concern. Oil immersed type reactors are immersed in a tank of oil for cooling purposes, making them more efficient in dissipating heat and suitable for outdoor installations. Both types have their own advantages and applications in the power industry.</p></p>
