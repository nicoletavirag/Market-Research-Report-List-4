<p><p>Bleached absorbent cotton is typically categorized into two market types: virgin cotton and noil. Virgin cotton refers to cotton fibers that have not been previously used or processed, making them pure and high in quality. Noil, on the other hand, consists of shorter fibers and small leftover pieces from the spinning process, resulting in a less refined product. Both types of cotton can be bleached to enhance their absorbency and cleanliness for use in various applications such as medical supplies, cosmetics, and hygiene products.</p></p>
