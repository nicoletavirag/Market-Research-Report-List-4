<p><p>Sterile Surgical Catgut market consists of two main types: Catgut and Chromic Catgut. Catgut is a type of natural collagen material derived from the submucosal layer of a sheep's intestines, while Chromic Catgut undergoes a process of chromium salt treatment to increase its tensile strength and slow down its absorption rate in the body. Catgut is commonly used for temporary wound closure and suturing in surgeries, while Chromic Catgut is preferred for procedures requiring prolonged suture retention.</p></p>
