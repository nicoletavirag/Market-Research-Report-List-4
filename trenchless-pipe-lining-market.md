<p><p>Trenchless pipe lining market offers various types of solutions for repairing and rehabilitating underground pipes without the need for extensive excavation. Types include Trenchless Sliplining, where a new pipe is inserted into the existing one; Cured-In-Place Pipe (CIPP), where a liner is inserted and inflated; Spray-In-Place Pipe (SIPP), where a polymer coating is sprayed onto the pipe interior; Pipe Bursting, where a new pipe is pulled through the old one, breaking it apart; and other innovative methods. These technologies help minimize disruption and save time and cost for pipe rehabilitation projects.</p></p>
