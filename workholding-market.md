<p><strong>The Workholding Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Milling/drilling workholding</li><li>Turning workholding</li><li>Grinding workholding</li><li>EDM workholding</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Workholding in machining can be divided into different types depending on the specific process being carried out. Milling/drilling workholding involves fixtures and clamps to securely hold workpieces during milling or drilling operations. Turning workholding utilizes chucks or collets to hold cylindrical workpieces during turning processes. Grinding workholding involves magnetic chucks or vices to secure workpieces during grinding operations. EDM workholding uses specialized fixtures to hold workpieces during electrical discharge machining processes. Each type of workholding is essential for ensuring accurate and efficient machining operations.</p></p>
