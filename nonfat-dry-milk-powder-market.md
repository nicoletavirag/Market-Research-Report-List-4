<p><strong>The Nonfat Dry Milk Powder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low Heat</li><li>Medium Heat</li><li>High Heat</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Nonfat dry milk powder is classified into three main types based on the heat treatment it undergoes during processing: low heat, medium heat, and high heat. Low heat powder is produced at temperatures below 160°F, preserving the milk's natural flavor and nutritional content. Medium heat powder is processed at temperatures between 176-194°F, while high heat powder is produced at temperatures exceeding 212°F. High heat powder has a more caramelized flavor and is typically used in baking applications, while low heat powder is ideal for beverages and other products where a mild, fresh milk flavor is desired.</p></p>
