<p><strong>The Copper Paste Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low Temperature Sintered</li><li>Medium Temperature Sintered</li><li>High Temperature Sintered</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Copper paste is categorized based on the temperature at which it is sintered. Low temperature sintered copper paste is processed at temperatures below 200°C, making it suitable for sensitive components. Medium temperature sintered copper paste is sintered between 200-300°C, providing a balance between conductivity and compatibility with various substrates. High temperature sintered copper paste is processed at temperatures above 300°C, offering superior conductivity and durability. These different types cater to varying requirements in industries such as electronics, automotive, and aerospace.</p></p>
