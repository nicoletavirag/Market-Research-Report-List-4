<p><strong>The Smart Connected HVAC Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Split Systems</li><li>Multi Split Systems</li><li>VRF Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Smart Connected HVAC systems are divided into three main types: Single Split Systems, Multi Split Systems, and VRF Systems. Single Split Systems consist of one indoor unit connected to one outdoor unit, making them ideal for small spaces. Multi Split Systems have multiple indoor units connected to one outdoor unit, providing flexibility for larger spaces. VRF Systems use advanced technology to regulate temperature in different zones simultaneously, making them suitable for commercial buildings or large residential complexes. All three types offer smart connectivity for remote control and energy efficiency.</p></p>
