<p><p>Weighted bars come in various types to suit different types of exercises. Standard bars are basic bars used for general weightlifting. Olympic weightlifting bars are designed for competitive weightlifting. Trap bars allow for a different grip and posture during deadlifts. Safety squat (yoke) bars are used for squatting with reduced strain on the shoulders. Cambered bars help with range of motion and stability during squats. Swiss bars are versatile for different grip positions. Curl bars (EZ-bars) are curved for easier grip during bicep curls.</p></p>
