<p><p>Rack Uninterruptible Power Supply (UPS) systems come in three main types: Static UPS, Dynamic UPS, and Hybrid UPS. Static UPS devices provide immediate power backup during outages using batteries. Dynamic UPS systems use flywheel energy storage to provide instantaneous power while switching to backup generators. Hybrid UPS units combine elements of both static and dynamic UPS systems to offer a more flexible and efficient power backup solution. Each type of UPS caters to different needs and preferences in the market, providing a range of options for businesses and organizations.</p></p>
