Tool calibration involves verifying and adjusting various tools such as torque wrenches, calipers, and micrometers to ensure they meet precision standards. On the other hand, gauge calibration focuses on verifying the accuracy of measuring instruments like pressure gauges, thermometers, and flow meters. Both markets play a crucial role in industries where precise measurements are essential, such as manufacturing, aerospace, and automotive sectors.</p></p>
