<p><h1>Poultry Plucker Market Size, Growth and Forecast from 2024 - 2031</h1></p><p><strong>Poultry Plucker Market Analysis and Latest Trends</strong></p>
<p><p>A poultry plucker is a machine used to remove feathers from poultry, such as chickens and ducks, after they have been slaughtered. The machine typically consists of rotating rubber fingers that grab and pull the feathers off the bird's body as it is passed through the machine. Poultry pluckers are commonly used by small-scale farmers, backyard poultry owners, and commercial poultry processors to streamline the feather removal process.</p><p>The Poultry Plucker Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.9% during the forecast period. This growth can be attributed to increasing demand for processed poultry products, growing poultry farming industry, and technological advancements in poultry processing equipment. Additionally, the rising disposable income of consumers and changing dietary preferences towards protein-rich food products are driving the demand for poultry pluckers.</p><p>One of the latest trends in the poultry plucker market is the development of automated plucking machines that can process a larger number of birds in a shorter period of time. These machines are designed to be more efficient, hygienic, and user-friendly, thereby increasing their adoption in poultry processing facilities. Moreover, manufacturers are focusing on producing compact and portable pluckers to cater to the needs of small-scale farmers and backyard poultry owners.</p></p>
