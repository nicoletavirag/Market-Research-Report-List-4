<p><p>Urology Imaging Systems market includes various types such as Conventional Radiology, Ultrasound, Magnetic Resonance Imaging, and Computed Tomography. Conventional Radiology utilizes X-rays to produce images of the urinary tract. Ultrasound uses sound waves to create images of the bladder and kidneys. Magnetic Resonance Imaging uses magnetic fields and radio waves to generate detailed images of the urinary system. Computed Tomography combines X-rays and computer technology to produce cross-sectional images of the urinary tract for accurate diagnosis and treatment planning.</p></p>
