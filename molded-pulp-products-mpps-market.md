<p><p>Molded Pulp Products (MPPs) are eco-friendly packaging solutions made from either recycled paper or pulp. Recycled paper made MPPs are created by processing used paper products into a pulp and molding it into various shapes for packaging. Pulp made MPPs are crafted from virgin pulp material, molding it into biodegradable and compostable packaging solutions. Both types offer sustainable alternatives to traditional plastic packaging, reducing environmental impact and promoting a circular economy.</p></p>
