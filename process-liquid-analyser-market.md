<p><p>Process liquid analyzers are used to monitor and analyze the composition of liquids in various industries. There are four main types of market technologies: NIR based analyzers use near-infrared light to determine chemical composition, NMR based analyzers use nuclear magnetic resonance technology, ECD sensor based analyzers utilize electrochemical detection sensors, and laser based analyzers employ laser technology for precise analysis. Each type of analyzer offers unique benefits and applications in different industries, providing accurate and real-time data for process control and optimization.</p></p>
