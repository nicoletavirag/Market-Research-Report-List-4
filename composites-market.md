<p><p>Composites market types include layup, filament winding, injection molding, pultrusion, and others. Layup involves layering sheets of fabric impregnated with resin. Filament winding uses continuous fibers wound around a mandrel. Injection molding involves injecting resin into a mold. Pultrusion pulls fibers through a resin bath before forming them into a shape. Other methods, such as compression molding and resin transfer molding, also play a role in the composites industry, offering various ways to create strong and lightweight materials.</p></p>
