<p><p>Cardiovascular ultrasound market includes several types of echocardiograms such as Transthoracic Echocardiogram, Transesophageal Echocardiogram, Stress Echocardiogram, and others. Transthoracic Echocardiogram involves placing the transducer on the chest to create images of the heart. Transesophageal Echocardiogram involves inserting a probe through the esophagus to get clearer images of the heart. Stress Echocardiogram is done during exercise to assess heart function. Other types of cardiovascular ultrasound include Doppler ultrasound and 3D echocardiography.</p></p>
