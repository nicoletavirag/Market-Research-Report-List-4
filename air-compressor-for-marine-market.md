<p><strong>The Air Compressor for Marine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Main Air Compressor</li><li>Topping Up Compressor</li><li>Deck Air Compressor</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Main air compressors are the primary source of compressed air on a marine vessel, used for various applications such as starting main engines, operating pneumatic tools, and controlling valves. Topping up compressors are smaller units used to supplement the main air compressor during peak demand periods. Deck air compressors are specifically designed for use on the deck of the ship for tasks like cleaning, painting, and maintenance. Other types of air compressors in the marine market include emergency air compressors and low-pressure air compressors for specific applications.</p></p>
