<p><p>The CO2 compressor market types include reciprocating compressors, multistage centrifugal compressors, reciprocating (piston) compressors, oil-lubricated screw compressors, and others. Reciprocating compressors use pistons to compress CO2, while multistage centrifugal compressors utilize multiple stages for higher pressure ratios. Reciprocating (piston) compressors are similar to reciprocating compressors but specifically use pistons for compression. Oil-lubricated screw compressors use screws to compress CO2 with the help of lubricating oil. Other types of CO2 compressors may include scroll compressors or rotary vane compressors.</p></p>
