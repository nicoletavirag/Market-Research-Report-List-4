<p><p>Flexible ENT endoscopes are available in two main types: single-use and reusable. Single-use endoscopes are designed for one-time use and are disposed of after each procedure, making them convenient and reducing the risk of cross-contamination. Reusable endoscopes, on the other hand, are sterilized and can be used multiple times, providing cost savings over time but requiring thorough cleaning and maintenance. Healthcare facilities can choose the type of endoscope that best suits their needs and budget.</p></p>
