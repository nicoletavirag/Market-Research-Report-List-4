<p><p>Deep Ultraviolet (DUV) Lithography Systems are advanced technology used for manufacturing semiconductors. There are three main types in the market - ArF Immersion, ArF Dry, and KrF. ArF Immersion lithography uses a liquid medium to improve resolution and precision. ArF Dry lithography operates in a dry environment without a liquid medium. KrF lithography is based on a shorter wavelength for even higher resolution. These different types cater to specific industry needs and requirements for producing high-quality semiconductor chips.</p></p>
