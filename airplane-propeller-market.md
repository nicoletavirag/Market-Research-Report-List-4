<p><p>Airplane propeller market can be divided into two main types: Fixed Pitch Propeller and Varying Pitch Propeller. Fixed Pitch Propellers have blades that are fixed at a certain angle and cannot be adjusted during flight, making them simpler and more cost-effective. On the other hand, Varying Pitch Propellers have blades that can be adjusted to change the angle of attack, allowing for better performance and efficiency in different flight conditions. Both types cater to different needs and preferences of aircraft manufacturers and operators.</p></p>
