<p><p>Desoldering braids, also known as desoldering wicks or solder wicks, are a type of braided copper wire used in electronics rework and repair to remove excess solder from a joint. They work by absorbing molten solder when pressed against the solder connection, effectively "wick" the solder away.</p><p>The desoldering braids market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7.9% during the forecast period. This growth can be attributed to the increasing demand for electronic devices and the need for efficient soldering and desoldering tools. With the rapid advancement in technology, there is a continuous need for delicate and precise soldering and desoldering processes, driving the demand for high-quality desoldering braids.</p><p>One of the latest trends in the desoldering braids market is the development of innovative braids that offer enhanced performance and durability. Manufacturers are focusing on improving the material composition and design of desoldering braids to ensure better solder absorption and heat resistance. Additionally, the rise in demand for lead-free soldering materials is also contributing to the growth of the desoldering braids market, as these braids are specially designed to work with lead-free solders. Overall, the desoldering braids market is poised for significant growth in the coming years.</p></p>
