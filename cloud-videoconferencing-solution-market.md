<p><p>Cloud video conferencing solutions can be classified into two main market types based on the video coding technology used - Scalable Video Coding (SVC) Type and Advanced Video Coding (AVC) Type. SVC systems allow for more scalability and adaptability in video quality, making them ideal for varying network conditions. On the other hand, AVC systems offer high-quality video compression but may not be as flexible as SVC systems. Organizations can choose the type of solution based on their specific needs and requirements for video conferencing.</p></p>
