<p><p>Addressable smoke detectors are classified into three main types: addressable photoelectric smoke detector, addressable thermal smoke detector, and addressable CO smoke detector. The addressable photoelectric smoke detector uses a light beam to detect smoke particles in the air. The addressable thermal smoke detector measures heat changes to identify the presence of smoke. The addressable CO smoke detector detects carbon monoxide levels in the environment. Each type of addressable smoke detector is designed to provide early and accurate detection of smoke or fire, ensuring prompt response and safety.</p></p>
