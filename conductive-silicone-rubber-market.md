<p><p>Conductive Silicone Rubber is available in two main types: Thermally Conductive and Electrically Conductive. Thermally conductive silicone rubber is designed to effectively dissipate heat, making it suitable for applications where thermal management is crucial. On the other hand, electrically conductive silicone rubber is formulated to conduct electricity, making it ideal for applications such as EMI shielding, grounding, and static dissipation. Both types of conductive silicone rubber offer unique properties that cater to specific needs in various industries.</p></p>
