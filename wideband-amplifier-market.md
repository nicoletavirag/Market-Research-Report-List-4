<p><p>Wideband amplifiers are categorized into two main types: Driver Amplifiers and Power Amplifiers. Driver amplifiers are designed to provide the necessary gain and power to drive the signal through the system, enabling higher performance and efficiency. On the other hand, Power amplifiers are responsible for amplifying the signal to a level suitable for output to the load, ensuring optimal performance and signal integrity. Both types are crucial components in various applications, including telecom, defense, aerospace, and industrial sectors.</p></p>
