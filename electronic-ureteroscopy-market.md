<p><p>The Electronic Ureteroscopy Market consists of two main types: Single-Use Ureteroscopes and Reusable Ureteroscopes. Reusable Ureteroscopes are sterilized and used multiple times, offering cost savings in the long run but requiring meticulous sterilization processes to ensure patient safety. Both types play a crucial role in minimally invasive procedures for diagnosing and treating conditions of the urinary tract.</p></p>
