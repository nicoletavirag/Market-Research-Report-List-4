<p><p>Commercial toilet tank fill valves come in two main types: float ball and float cup. The float ball fill valve uses a traditional spherical float attached to a rod that rises and falls with water levels in the tank to regulate water flow. On the other hand, the float cup fill valve features a cup-shaped float that moves up and down to control the flow of water. Both types are commonly used in commercial settings to ensure proper filling and water conservation in toilet tanks.</p></p>
