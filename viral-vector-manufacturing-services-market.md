<p><p>Viral vector manufacturing services cater to different types of viral vectors, including retroviral vectors, adenoviral vectors, adeno-associated viral vectors, and other viral vectors. Retroviral vectors are derived from retroviruses and are commonly used for gene therapy applications. Adenoviral vectors are derived from adenoviruses and are known for their ability to efficiently deliver genes into target cells. Adeno-associated viral vectors are derived from adeno-associated viruses and are popular for their low immunogenicity and long-lasting gene expression. Other viral vectors include lentiviral vectors, herpes simplex vectors, and vaccinia vectors.</p></p>
