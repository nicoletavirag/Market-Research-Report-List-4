<p><p>Animal feed prebiotics market types include inulin, fructooligosaccharide (FOS), isomaltooligosaccharide (IMO), and others. Inulin is a soluble dietary fiber derived from plants, FOS are naturally occurring carbohydrates found in fruits and vegetables, and IMO is a type of prebiotic made from starch. These prebiotics help improve gut health in animals by promoting the growth of beneficial bacteria in the digestive tract, leading to better nutrient absorption and overall health.</p></p>
