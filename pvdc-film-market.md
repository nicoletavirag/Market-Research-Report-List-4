<p><strong>The PVDC Film Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Emulsion Polymerized PVDC Film</li><li>Suspension Polymerized PVDC Film</li></ul></p>
<p><p>PVDC film is available in two main types: Emulsion Polymerized PVDC Film and Suspension Polymerized PVDC Film. Emulsion Polymerized PVDC Film is made by emulsifying PVDC resin in water with detergent and polymerizing it through a chemical reaction. This type of film offers excellent barrier properties against oxygen and moisture. Suspension Polymerized PVDC Film is produced by suspending PVDC resin particles in water and polymerizing them through a reaction. This type of film is known for its superior heat resistance and clarity. Both types of PVDC films are widely used in packaging applications.</p></p>
