<p><ul><li>Single-Tube</li><li>Double Tube</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive hydraulic buffer market consists of two main types: single-tube and double-tube buffers. Single-tube buffers have a simpler design with a single tube containing the hydraulic fluid and piston mechanism. Double-tube buffers, on the other hand, have an additional tube surrounding the inner tube to provide extra protection and stability. Single-tube buffers are typically more cost-effective and easier to install, while double-tube buffers offer improved durability and performance in more demanding applications.</p></p>
