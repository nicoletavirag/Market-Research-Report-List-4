ZigBee-based bulbs operate on a mesh network, allowing for long-range coverage and low power consumption. Wi-Fi bulbs offer seamless integration with existing home networks, providing easy control through smartphone apps. Bluetooth bulbs are convenient for short-range control, making them ideal for smaller spaces. Each market type offers unique benefits to cater to different user preferences.</p></p>
