<p><p>CFL (Compact Fluorescent Lighting), LFL (Linear Fluorescent Lighting), HID (High-Intensity Discharge) and LED (Light Emitting Diode) are different types of lighting commonly used in the hospitality industry. CFL and LFL are energy-efficient options that provide bright, consistent light. HID lights are powerful and long-lasting, making them suitable for outdoor use. LED lights are the most modern and energy-efficient option, offering high-quality lighting with low energy consumption. Each type of lighting has unique benefits and is chosen based on the specific needs of the hospitality environment.</p></p>
