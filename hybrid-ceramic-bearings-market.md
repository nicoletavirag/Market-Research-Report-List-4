<p><p>Hybrid ceramic bearings market is categorized into two types based on the material used: Si3N4 and non-Si3N4. Si3N4 material bearings are known for their high strength, durability, and resistance to corrosion, making them ideal for high-performance applications. On the other hand, non-Si3N4 material bearings are typically made of steel or other metals, offering a more cost-effective option for less demanding applications. Both types of bearings cater to diverse market needs by providing different combinations of performance, cost, and durability.</p></p>
