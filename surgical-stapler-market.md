<p><p>Surgical staplers are commonly used in various surgical procedures, and they can be categorized into two main types: laparoscopic staplers and open staplers. Laparoscopic staplers are used in minimally invasive procedures where a small incision is made, and a camera is used to guide the stapler. Open staplers, on the other hand, are used in traditional open surgeries where a larger incision is made. Both types of staplers provide efficient and reliable closure of tissues during surgical procedures.</p></p>
