Centrifugal separators work by utilizing centrifugal force to separate the different components of the pipeline stream, while filter separators use various types of filters to remove impurities from the stream. Both types of separators play a crucial role in ensuring the smooth operation and efficiency of pipeline systems in the oil and gas industry.</p></p>
