Flat-bed screen printing equipment is ideal for printing on flat surfaces such as paper and cardboard. Cylinder screen printing equipment is suitable for printing on cylindrical objects like bottles and cups. Rotary screen printing equipment is used for high-speed, continuous printing on fabrics and other materials. Each type caters to different printing needs and industries, offering a range of capabilities and efficiencies.</p></p>
