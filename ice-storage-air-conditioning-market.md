<p><p>Ice storage air conditioning systems can be divided into two main market types: static ice making and dynamic ice making. </p><p>Static ice making systems involve producing and storing a large amount of ice during off-peak hours to be used for cooling during peak demand periods. Dynamic ice making systems, on the other hand, continuously produce ice in real-time based on current cooling needs, offering more flexibility and efficiency. Both types of systems aim to reduce energy costs and peak electricity demand while providing effective and sustainable cooling solutions.</p></p>
