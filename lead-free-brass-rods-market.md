<p><p>The lead free brass rods market can be categorized into four types based on their composition: Cu-Zn-Bi, Cu-Zn-Si, Cu-Zn-Sb, and others. Cu-Zn-Bi brass rods are made by adding bismuth to improve machinability and corrosion resistance. Cu-Zn-Si brass rods contain silicon for improved strength and wear resistance. Cu-Zn-Sb brass rods have antimony added for enhanced mechanical properties. The "others" category includes brass rods with different alloy compositions tailored to specific applications or performance requirements.</p></p>
