<p><strong>The CO2 Gas Sensor Module Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>NDIR (Non-Dispersive Infrared) CO2 Sensors</li><li>EC (Electrochemical) CO2 Sensors</li><li>PAS (Photoacoustic Spectroscopy) CO2 Sensors</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>NDIR (Non-Dispersive Infrared) CO2 Sensors are based on the principle of measuring the absorption of infrared radiation by CO2 molecules. EC (Electrochemical) CO2 Sensors use a chemical reaction to detect CO2 levels. PAS (Photoacoustic Spectroscopy) CO2 Sensors measure the sound waves generated by the absorption of light by CO2 molecules. Other types of CO2 gas sensor modules may include solid-state sensors or colorimetric sensors, each utilizing different mechanisms for detecting and measuring CO2 levels in the atmosphere.</p></p>
