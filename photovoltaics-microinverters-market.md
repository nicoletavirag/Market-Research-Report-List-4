<p><ul><li>Grid-Connected PV Microinverter</li><li>Grid-Off PV Microinverter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Grid-connected PV microinverters are designed to convert the DC power produced by solar panels into AC power that can be fed back into the grid. These microinverters are used in commercial and residential applications where electricity can be generated and utilized on-site, with any excess power being sold back to the grid.</p><p>On the other hand, grid-off PV microinverters are used in off-grid applications where no connection to the grid is available. These microinverters store excess power in batteries for later use, making them ideal for remote locations or areas with unreliable grid access.</p></p>
