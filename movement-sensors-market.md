<p><p>Active movement sensors use emitted energy to detect changes in their surroundings, while passive movement sensors rely on detecting changes in the environment without emitting energy. Dual technology sensors combine both active and passive technologies to provide more accurate and reliable detection. Active sensors are more suitable for outdoor applications where consistent monitoring is needed, while passive sensors are commonly used indoors for their cost-effectiveness. Dual technology sensors are often used in high-security environments where accuracy is crucial.</p></p>
