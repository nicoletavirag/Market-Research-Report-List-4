<p><ul><li>Monorail</li><li>Double Track</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Car Lift Rail market includes both monorail and double track systems. Monorail systems consist of a single track that supports the weight of the car lift mechanism, allowing for efficient movement and storage of vehicles in tight spaces. Double track systems, on the other hand, have two parallel tracks that provide added stability and support for heavier vehicles. Both types of systems are commonly used in car parking facilities and automotive workshops to maximize space and streamline vehicle storage and maintenance processes.</p></p>
