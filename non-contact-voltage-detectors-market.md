<p><p>Non-contact voltage detectors come in two main types: capacitive and magnetic induction. Capacitive voltage detectors use the principle of detecting changes in an electric field around a live conductor, while magnetic induction detectors rely on the principle of detecting changes in a magnetic field around a live conductor. Both types of detectors are widely used in various industries for safety purposes, allowing users to quickly and safely determine the presence of voltage without making direct contact with the electrical source.</p></p>
