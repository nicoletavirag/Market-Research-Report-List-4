<p><p>Tunneled catheters are classified into different types based on the number of lumens they possess. Single-lumen catheters have one tube for delivering fluids, medications, or withdrawing blood. Double-lumen catheters have two lumens, allowing for separate infusion and withdrawal channels. Triple-lumen catheters have three lumens, providing even more versatility in medical procedures. Other types of tunneled catheters may have more than three lumens or different configurations to suit specific clinical requirements. Each type offers distinct advantages for various medical treatments.</p></p>
