<p><ul><li>Full Height Closed Automatic Platform Gates</li><li>Half Height Open Automatic Platform Gates</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Full Height Closed Automatic Platform Gates are gates that extend from floor to ceiling, enclosing the entire platform area. They provide maximum security by preventing unauthorized access and ensuring only passengers with valid tickets can enter and exit the platform. In contrast, Half Height Open Automatic Platform Gates are gates that only cover the lower half of the platform, allowing for easier passenger movement and a more open feel. They are typically used in stations with lower security requirements or where quick access is needed.</p></p>
