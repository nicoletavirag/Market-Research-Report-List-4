<p><p>Integrated Distribution Box Market includes two main types: DC Distribution Box and AC Distribution Box. </p><p>DC Distribution Boxes are designed to distribute direct current (DC) power from sources like solar panels or batteries to various devices and appliances. They are commonly used in off-grid or hybrid solar power systems.</p><p>AC Distribution Boxes, on the other hand, are used to distribute alternating current (AC) power from the main electrical grid to different circuits within a building. They are essential for ensuring proper distribution and protection of electrical circuits in homes and commercial buildings.</p></p>
