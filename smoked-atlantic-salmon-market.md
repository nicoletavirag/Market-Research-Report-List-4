<p><p>Smoked Atlantic salmon can be found in two main market types: hot smoking and cold smoking. Hot smoking involves cooking the salmon at a higher temperature, resulting in a firmer texture and a more pronounced smoky flavor. On the other hand, cold smoking is done at a lower temperature, preserving the delicate texture of the fish and providing a milder smoky taste. Both methods are popular choices for preparing smoked salmon and offer different flavor profiles to suit individual preferences.</p></p>
