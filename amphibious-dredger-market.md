<p><p>Amphibious dredgers come in various types, with the main ones being cutter-suction dredgers, trailing suction hopper dredgers, and others. Cutter-suction dredgers are equipped with a rotating cutting head to break up and remove material, while trailing suction hopper dredgers use a trailing suction pipe to suck up material and transport it to a disposal site. Other types of amphibious dredgers may include bucket dredgers and grab dredgers, each with specific capabilities and applications in dredging operations.</p></p>
