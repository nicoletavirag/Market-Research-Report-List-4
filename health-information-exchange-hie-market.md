<p><p>Health Information Exchange (HIE) Market Application includes three key approaches: Directed Exchange, Query-based Exchange, and Consumer Mediated Exchange. Directed Exchange involves the secure exchange of health information between healthcare providers for treatment purposes. Query-based Exchange allows providers to request specific patient information from other healthcare organizations. Consumer Mediated Exchange empowers patients to control and manage their own health information by sharing it securely with their healthcare providers. These applications aim to improve coordination of care, enhance patient engagement, and facilitate seamless sharing of health information.</p></p>
