<p><strong>The Temporary Cement Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Eugenol-based</li><li>Non-eugenol Based</li><li>Resin Based</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Temporary cement is available in various types based on their composition and properties. Eugenol-based cements contain eugenol, which can have a sedative effect on the pulp. Non-eugenol based cements do not contain eugenol and are preferred for patients with allergies. Resin-based cements provide superior strength and durability. Other types of temporary cements may include zinc oxide-based cements or glass ionomer cements. Each type of temporary cement offers unique advantages and is chosen based on the specific needs of the patient and the procedure.</p></p>
