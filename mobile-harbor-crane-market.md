<p><ul><li>Gantry Crane</li><li>Half Gantry Crane</li><li>Double Cantilever Gantry Crane</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mobile harbor cranes are classified into three main types: Gantry Crane, Half Gantry Crane, and Double Cantilever Gantry Crane. Gantry cranes are supported on parallel runways with a traveling mechanism that spans the gap. Half Gantry Cranes have one end supported on a wall or building, while the other end travels on rails. Double Cantilever Gantry Cranes have two cantilever beams that allow for extended reach and increased lifting capacity. Each type caters to specific port logistics needs and operational requirements.</p></p>
