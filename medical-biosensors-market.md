<p><p>Medical biosensors are devices that detect specific biological parameters. They can be categorized into different types based on the sense they use to detect these parameters. Vision type biosensors rely on imaging technology to detect changes in the body, while hearing type biosensors use sound signals. Smell type biosensors utilize olfactory receptors to detect certain compounds. There are also other emerging markets for biosensors, such as touch and taste sensors, which are currently in development. Each type of biosensor offers unique advantages and applications in the medical field.</p></p>
