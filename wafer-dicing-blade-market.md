<p><strong>The Wafer Dicing Blade Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hub Dicing Blades</li><li>Hubless Dicing Blades</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hub dicing blades are wheels with a metal core that attaches to a hub. They are commonly used for cutting hard and brittle materials like silicon wafers. Hubless dicing blades, on the other hand, do not have a hub and are directly mounted to the dicing machine. They are preferred for cutting softer materials like plastics and metals. Both types of dicing blades are crucial in the semiconductor industry for precise wafer dicing and are available in various sizes and thicknesses to meet specific cutting requirements.</p></p>
