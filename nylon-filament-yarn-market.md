<p><p>Nylon filament yarn is categorized into four main types: flat, partially oriented yarn (POY), fully drawn yarn (FDY), and high-speed spinning oriented (HSO) market. Flat yarns have a smooth surface and are commonly used for weaving. POY yarns have low elasticity and are suitable for high-speed texturing processes. FDY yarns are highly uniform and strong, making them ideal for applications that require durability. HSO yarns are produced at high speeds, resulting in improved strength and elasticity for various textile products.</p></p>
