<p><strong>The High Performance PTFE Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Granular</li><li>Aqueous Dispersion</li><li>Micronized</li></ul></p>
<p><p>High Performance PTFE market can be categorized into three main types based on their form: Granular, Aqueous Dispersion, and Micronized. Granular PTFE is in solid form and is commonly used for molding and extrusion processes. Aqueous Dispersion PTFE is in liquid form and provides better flow and surface coverage. Micronized PTFE is a fine powder form with smaller particle size, offering improved dispersion and lubrication properties. Each type caters to different industrial applications based on their unique characteristics and properties.</p></p>
