<p><p>BBQ seasoning market can be categorized into two main types: dry seasoning and wet seasoning. Dry seasoning typically consists of a blend of spices, herbs, salt, and sugar, which is applied directly to the meat before cooking to enhance its flavor. Wet seasoning, on the other hand, refers to pastes or sauces that are used to marinate the meat before grilling. Both types of seasonings are popular choices for adding flavor to BBQ dishes and can cater to different preferences and cooking styles.</p></p>
