<p><strong>The Modular Belt Conveyor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open Belt Conveyor</li><li>Enclosed Belt Conveyor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The modular belt conveyor market can be divided into two types: open belt conveyors and enclosed belt conveyors. Open belt conveyors have a simple design with a belt running along rollers, suitable for transporting bulk materials. Enclosed belt conveyors have protective casing around the belt, minimizing dust and spillage while ensuring the safety of conveyed goods. Both types offer efficient material handling solutions for various industries, with open belt conveyors being more cost-effective and enclosed belt conveyors providing a cleaner and safer transport environment.</p></p>
