<p><p>Hyperspectral infrared cameras come in four main types: Visible/Near Infrared (VNIR), Short Wave Infrared (SWIR), Medium Wave Infrared (MWIR), and Long Wave Infrared (LWIR). VNIR cameras capture light in the visible and near-infrared spectrum, SWIR cameras detect wavelengths slightly longer than VNIR, MWIR cameras detect mid-infrared wavelengths, and LWIR cameras detect longer infrared wavelengths. Each type of camera has its own unique applications and advantages, catering to different industries and research fields.</p></p>
