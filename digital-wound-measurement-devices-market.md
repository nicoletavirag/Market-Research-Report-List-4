<p><p>Digital Wound Measurement Devices Market can be categorized into two types - Contact Type and Non-Contact Type. Contact Type devices require physical contact with the wound to measure its dimensions, which may provide more accurate results but can be invasive for the patient. On the other hand, Non-Contact Type devices can measure the dimensions of the wound without touching it, offering less discomfort for the patient but may have slightly less accuracy compared to Contact Type devices.</p></p>
