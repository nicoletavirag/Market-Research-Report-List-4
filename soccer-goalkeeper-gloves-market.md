Rolled/gunn gloves feature rolled fingers that provide excellent grip and ball control. Negative cut gloves have negative stitching that offers a snug fit and enhanced feel. Hybrid cut gloves combine elements of different cuts for a customized fit and excellent performance. Each market type offers unique benefits and caters to different preferences of goalkeepers.</p></p>
