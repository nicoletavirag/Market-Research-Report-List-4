<p><strong>The Handheld 3D Scanner Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Structure Light Scanner</li><li>Laser Scanner</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Handheld 3D scanners use different technologies for capturing three-dimensional objects. Structure Light Scanners work by projecting a pattern of light onto the object and using a camera to analyze the distortion of the pattern, creating a 3D model. Laser Scanners, on the other hand, use laser beams to measure the distance between the scanner and the object, creating a precise 3D model. Both types of scanners are used in various industries such as industrial design, healthcare, and entertainment for purposes like quality control, reverse engineering, and digitalization.</p></p>
