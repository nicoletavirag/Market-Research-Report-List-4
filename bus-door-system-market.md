<p><p>Bus door systems come in two main types: Electrical Actuator Systems and Pneumatic Actuator Systems. Electrical Actuator Systems use electric motors to operate the bus doors, providing a reliable and efficient solution. Pneumatic Actuator Systems, on the other hand, use compressed air to open and close the bus doors. Each system has its advantages and can be suitable for different bus door configurations and usage requirements. Both systems play a crucial role in ensuring safe and convenient boarding and disembarking for passengers.</p></p>
