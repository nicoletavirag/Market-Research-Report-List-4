<p><p>Allergic conjunctivitis treatments market includes two main types: acute allergic conjunctivitis and chronic allergic conjunctivitis. Acute allergic conjunctivitis is a temporary condition that occurs as a result of exposure to allergens, and typically resolves on its own or with the use of over-the-counter antihistamine eye drops. Chronic allergic conjunctivitis, on the other hand, is a long-term condition that requires ongoing treatment with prescription medications such as corticosteroids or immunomodulators to manage symptoms and prevent flare-ups.</p></p>
