<p><p>Elemental Boron is available in two main market types: Amorphous Boron and Crystalline Boron. Amorphous Boron is a non-crystalline form of elemental boron which has irregular and random atomic structure. It is mainly used in pyrotechnic applications and electronic devices. On the other hand, Crystalline Boron has a regular atomic structure with strong bonds between atoms. It is utilized in ceramic and refractory industries due to its high strength and resistance to heat. Both types of elemental boron have distinct properties and applications in various industries.</p></p>
