<p><p>The DPM Barcode Reader market consists of two main types: stationary and handheld devices. Handheld readers, on the other hand, are portable devices that can be carried around to scan barcodes on various objects. While stationary readers are ideal for high-volume scanning operations, handheld readers offer more flexibility and convenience for mobile scanning tasks.
