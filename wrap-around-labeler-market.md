<p><p>Wrap around labelers come in two main types: automatic and semi-automatic. Automatic wrap around labelers are fully automated machines that apply labels to products with minimal human intervention. They are ideal for high-volume production lines where speed and accuracy are crucial. Semi-automatic wrap around labelers, on the other hand, require some manual involvement in placing the products before the labels are applied. This type is more suitable for smaller production runs or for products that require more precise positioning of the labels.</p></p>
