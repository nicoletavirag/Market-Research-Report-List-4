<p><p>Solid state gyroscopes are used for measuring angular rotation in various applications. They come in different market types based on the number of axes they can measure: single-axis, dual-axis, and tri-axis. Single-axis gyroscopes measure rotation around one axis, dual-axis gyroscopes measure rotation around two axes, and tri-axis gyroscopes measure rotation around all three axes. Each type has specific advantages and is used in different applications based on the level of accuracy and complexity required for the measurement of angular rotation.</p></p>
