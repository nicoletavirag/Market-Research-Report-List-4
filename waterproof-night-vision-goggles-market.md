<p><p>Waterproof night vision goggles are available in two main types: Image Intensifier and Thermal Image market. Image intensifier goggles amplify ambient light to produce a visible image in low-light conditions. They are ideal for use in urban environments. Thermal image goggles detect heat signatures to create images, making them effective in total darkness or through obstacles like smoke. They are commonly used in military and law enforcement operations. Both types of goggles are designed to withstand water exposure, making them suitable for use in various outdoor environments.</p></p>
