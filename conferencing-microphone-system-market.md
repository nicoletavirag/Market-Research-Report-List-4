<p><p>Conferencing Microphone Systems come in two main types: Wired and Wireless. Wired systems require a physical connection between the microphones and the audio interface, providing reliable and stable audio quality. Wireless systems, on the other hand, offer more flexibility and mobility as they operate through radio frequency signals, eliminating the need for cables. Both types cater to different preferences and requirements, with wired systems being ideal for fixed installations and wireless systems offering convenience for dynamic settings.</p></p>
