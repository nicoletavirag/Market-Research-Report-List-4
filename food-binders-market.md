They can be categorized into four main types including sugar binders, starch binders, protein binders, and gel binders. Sugar binders are used primarily for sweetness and moisture retention, starch binders for thickening and setting, protein binders for structure and elasticity, and gel binders for gelling and coagulation properties. Each type offers unique functionalities that cater to specific food applications and preferences in the market.</p></p>
