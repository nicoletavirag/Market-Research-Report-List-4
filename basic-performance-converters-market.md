<p><ul><li>Single-stage Torque Converter</li><li>Multistage Torque Converter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Single-stage Torque Converters consist of a single turbine and stator set, providing basic torque multiplication. They are commonly used in smaller vehicles for cost efficiency. Multistage Torque Converters, on the other hand, have multiple sets of turbines and stators, offering more refined and efficient torque conversion. They are typically found in larger vehicles or vehicles with higher torque requirements. Both types play a crucial role in the transmission system of vehicles by optimizing the transfer of power from the engine to the wheels.</p></p>
