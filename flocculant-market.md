<p><p>Flocculant market is categorized into three types - Inorganic Flocculant, Organic Flocculant, and Others. Inorganic flocculants are synthetic chemicals like aluminum sulfate and ferric chloride that are used for solid-liquid separation in wastewater treatment. Organic flocculants are natural or synthetic polymers that aid in the aggregation of suspended particles. The "Others" category includes specialty flocculants like microbial flocculants or bio-based flocculants. Each type of flocculant offers unique properties and benefits for various industrial and municipal applications.</p></p>
