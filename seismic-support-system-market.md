<p><p>Seismic support systems are used to protect pipelines, structures, and equipment from the damaging effects of earthquakes and other seismic events. Three common types of seismic support systems are cylindrical support, bushing support, and conical support. Cylindrical supports provide vertical support while allowing for lateral movement, bushing supports absorb vibrations and allow for movement in multiple directions, and conical supports offer a combination of vertical and lateral support. Each type of support system is designed to meet specific seismic protection needs.</p></p>
