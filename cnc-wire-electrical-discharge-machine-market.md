<p><p>CNC Wire Electrical Discharge Machine market can be categorized into three types: Flush Type, Submerged Type, and Others. Flush type machines have a nozzle that flushes dielectric fluid over the workpiece during machining. Submerged type machines have the workpiece submerged in dielectric fluid during operation. The Other category includes machines with unique features not fitting into the other two types. Each type offers distinct advantages and is preferred based on specific machining requirements and preferences of the operators.</p></p>
