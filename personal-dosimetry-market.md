<p><p>Personal Dosimetry market is categorized into different types based on the detection technology used. Thermoluminescent Dosimeters (TLD) measure exposure to ionizing radiation by detecting light emissions when heated. Optically Stimulated Luminescence (OSL) dosimeters use light to measure radiation exposure. Radiation Protection Dosimeters (RPL) monitor dose equivalent for radiation workers. Active type dosimeters utilize real-time monitoring technology to continuously measure and record radiation exposure. Each type offers unique features and benefits to suit various monitoring needs in different industries.</p></p>
