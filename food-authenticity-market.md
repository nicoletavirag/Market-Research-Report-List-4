PCR-Based market relies on DNA analysis to detect species-specific markers, while LC-MS/MS market uses mass spectrometry to analyze chemical compounds in food samples. Isotope market measures the stable isotopes present in food to verify their geographical origin. These markets play a crucial role in preventing food fraud and ensuring consumer confidence in the authenticity of food products.</p></p>
