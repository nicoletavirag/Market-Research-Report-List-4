<p><ul><li>Palm Oil Type</li><li>Lauric Oil Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Butter alternatives are divided into two main types based on the primary ingredients used: Palm Oil Type and Lauric Oil Type. Palm Oil Type alternatives are made primarily from palm oil, which has a high saturated fat content and provides a similar texture to butter. On the other hand, Lauric Oil Type alternatives are made from oils with a high percentage of lauric acid, such as coconut and palm kernel oil, providing a creamy texture and rich flavor similar to butter. Both types cater to consumers looking for dairy-free or vegan options.</p></p>
