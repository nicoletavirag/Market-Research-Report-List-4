<p><p>Automated hematology analyzers come in two main types: semi-automated and fully automated. Semi-automated analyzers are often preferred for smaller labs with lower testing volumes, while fully automated analyzers are more suitable for high-throughput labs requiring faster and more efficient testing processes.
