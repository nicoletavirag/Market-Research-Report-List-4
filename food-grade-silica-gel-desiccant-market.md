<p><ul><li>Fine-Pored Silica Gel</li><li>Mesoporous Silica Gel</li><li>Coarse Pore Silica Gel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Food grade silica gel desiccant is available in three main types: fine-pored silica gel, mesoporous silica gel, and coarse pore silica gel. Fine-pored silica gel has a high capacity for moisture absorption due to its small pores. Mesoporous silica gel has medium-sized pores that allow for a balance between absorption capacity and speed. Coarse pore silica gel has larger pores and is ideal for applications where fast moisture absorption is required. Each type offers unique benefits for different food packaging and preservation needs.</p></p>
