<p><p>Medical solid waste treatment market includes various types of treatment methods such as incineration, landfill, autoclave, chemical treatment, and others. Autoclave uses steam and pressure to sterilize the waste. Chemical treatment involves using disinfectants or chemical agents to neutralize the waste. Other treatments may include microwave treatment, steam sterilization, or shredding.</p></p>
