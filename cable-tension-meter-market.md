<p><p>Cable tension meters are available in three main types: handheld, hanging, and vertical. Handheld tension meters are designed to be held in the hand and are typically portable and easy to use. Hanging tension meters are suspended from a cable or wire and can measure tension in a vertical direction. Vertical tension meters are mounted vertically on a structure or piece of equipment to measure tension. Each type of tension meter is suited to different applications and environments, providing accurate tension measurements for various industries.</p></p>
