<p><ul><li>Split Type</li><li>Straight Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fire elevator markets can be categorized into two main types: Split Type and Straight Type. Split Type markets involve elevators that are specifically designed to split into two separate compartments in the event of a fire, allowing for safe evacuation of passengers. On the other hand, Straight Type markets feature elevators with a single compartment that remains intact during a fire, providing a secure means for firefighting personnel to access different floors. Both types cater to different safety needs in the event of a fire emergency.</p></p>
