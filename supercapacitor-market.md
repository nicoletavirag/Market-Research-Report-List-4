<p><strong>The Supercapacitor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Double layer capacitors</li><li>Pseudocapacitors</li><li>Hybrid capacitors</li><li>Composite Hybrids</li><li>Asymmetric Hybrids</li><li>Battery-type Hybrids</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The supercapacitor market can be divided into different types based on their energy storage mechanism. Double layer capacitors store energy through electrostatic double-layer capacitance, while pseudocapacitors store energy through fast and reversible faradaic reactions. Hybrid capacitors combine features of both double layer and pseudocapacitors. Composite hybrids use a combination of different materials to enhance energy storage efficiency. Asymmetric hybrids have electrodes with different capacitance mechanisms. Battery-type hybrids combine supercapacitor and battery technologies for improved performance and energy storage capabilities.</p></p>
