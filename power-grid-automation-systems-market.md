<p><p>Power grid automation systems can be categorized into two main types: On-grid automation systems and Off-grid automation systems. On-grid automation systems are designed to monitor and control power distribution on a centralized power grid network, ensuring efficiency and reliability. Off-grid automation systems, on the other hand, are used in isolated or remote areas where grid connections are not available. They help manage power generation and distribution locally, ensuring reliable electricity supply in off-grid locations.</p></p>
