<p><p>Oil-free Pressure Booster market can be categorized into two types: Water Cooled and Air Cooled. Water Cooled pressure boosters use water as a coolant to dissipate heat generated during operation, while Air Cooled pressure boosters use air for the same purpose. Water Cooled systems are known for their higher efficiency and lower noise levels, making them ideal for indoor applications. On the other hand, Air Cooled systems are more compact and suitable for outdoor installations where water availability is limited.</p></p>
