<p><p>Automatic Transmission Oil Pump Market consists of three main types: Internal Gear Oil Pump, Rotary Oil Pump, and Blade Oil Pump. </p><p>Internal Gear Oil Pump uses two gears to pump oil, creating suction and discharge in a compact design. Rotary Oil Pump uses a rotating component to draw in and expel oil in a continuous flow. Blade Oil Pump utilizes blades to push oil through the pump, providing high efficiency and reliability. Each type offers unique benefits and is used in different transmission systems based on specific requirements.</p></p>
