Line Scan Cameras capture images line by line, ideal for inspecting continuous objects like web materials. Area Scan Cameras capture images in a defined area at once, suitable for stationary objects. Both types of cameras cater to specific industrial needs for high-speed and accurate imaging in various applications such as quality control, process monitoring, and machine vision.</p></p>
