<p><ul><li>Low viscosity Liquid Epoxy Resin</li><li>High viscosity Liquid Epoxy Resin</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Liquid Epoxy Resins market can be divided into two main types based on viscosity: Low viscosity and High viscosity. Low viscosity Liquid Epoxy Resins are more fluid and easily flowable, making them ideal for applications where a thin coating or penetration is needed. High viscosity Liquid Epoxy Resins, on the other hand, are thicker and provide better gap filling properties, making them suitable for applications where a thicker layer or higher build-up is required. Both types offer unique advantages depending on the specific requirements of a project.</p></p>
