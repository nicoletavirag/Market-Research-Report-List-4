<p><p>Stainless steel abrasive market includes two main types: stainless steel shot and stainless steel grit. Stainless steel shot is a rounded abrasive material used for peening and polishing applications. It is known for its durability and recyclability. On the other hand, stainless steel grit is angular in shape and is used for surface preparation and cleaning applications. Both types of abrasives are highly effective in removing surface contaminants and producing a smooth finish on various materials.</p></p>
